Filipino Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fruit cocktail, drained
  • 1 cup nata de coco, drained
  • 1 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit), drained
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 carton (8 ounces) all-purpose cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the drained fruit cocktail, nata de coco, and kaong.
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and all-purpose cream. Mix gently until evenly coated.
  3. If desired, add shredded cheese and gently fold into the mixture.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until well chilled.
  5. Serve cold and enjoy this classic Filipino dessert!

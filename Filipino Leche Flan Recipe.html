Filipino Leche Flan

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (for caramel)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the sugar until it turns into a golden caramel. Quickly pour it into your mold (llanera) and spread evenly. Set aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Gently mix until smooth but avoid creating too many bubbles.
  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared mold with the caramel.
  4. Cover the mold with aluminum foil to prevent water from getting into the flan.
  5. Steam the flan over medium heat for about 30-35 minutes or until set. You can also bake it in a water bath at 350°F (175°C) for the same duration.
  6. Allow the flan to cool completely before refrigerating for at least 2 hours.
  7. To serve, run a knife around the edges of the mold, invert onto a serving plate, and enjoy!

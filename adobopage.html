Delicious Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine the chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaf, and peppercorns in a large bowl. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pork that cut into pieces and cook until the chicken is browned on all sides.
  3. Add water to cover the pork and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.

Filipino Buko Pandan

Ingredients

  • 2 cups young coconut strips (buko)
  • 1 pack (25g) pandan-flavored gelatin
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup nata de coco, drained
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 carton (8 ounces) all-purpose cream
  • 1/4 cup small tapioca pearls (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pandan-flavored gelatin according to the package instructions. Once set, cut into small cubes.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the coconut strips, nata de coco, and tapioca pearls (if using).
  3. Add the pandan gelatin cubes to the bowl.
  4. Pour in the sweetened condensed milk and all-purpose cream. Mix gently until well combined.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to chill.
  6. Serve cold and enjoy this refreshing Filipino dessert!

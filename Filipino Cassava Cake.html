Filipino Cassava Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated cassava
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (for topping)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish or tray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine grated cassava, coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, eggs, and sugar. Mix until well blended.
  3. Pour the mixture into the greased baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or until the top is slightly firm.
  5. Remove from the oven and sprinkle grated cheese evenly on top.
  6. Return the dish to the oven and bake for another 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  7. Allow the cassava cake to cool before slicing into squares. Serve and enjoy!

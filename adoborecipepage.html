Delicious Pork Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the pork belly in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and black peppercorns for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the marinated pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add the sliced onion, bay leaves, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  4. Cover and cook for 40-50 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pork is tender and the sauce has reduced.
  5. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
